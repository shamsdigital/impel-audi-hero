Opportunities in a Tight Market

Attempting to respond to every inquiry, follow up with each lead, send and answer emails, handle routine questions set up appointments, and track every channel (while delivering a great experience for every shopper) was straining team resources - and impeding their ability to prioritize ready-to-buy customers. Increasingly, the team had to make judgment calls about which opportunities to chase and which leads to drop.
